Sleepy Star

The Sleepy Star Baby's sleep tracker is a digital application which provides parents tools needed to track and monitor their baby's sleep schedule.

By tracking their baby's sleep, parents ensure their babies are getting enough sleep which will ultimately help them sleep through the night. Getting a good nights sleep is essential to your child's growth and development and it is also essential for you.

Created by Nicole Dagnenica, the Sleepy Star App focuses solely on sleep. Sleepy Star started eleven years ago when Nicole’s first child, Julie, was born. Nicole found sleep deprivation, like most parents, very difficult. She became knowledgeable about babies sleep and created a sleep chart to keep track of her children’s sleep.

Nicole had the idea to turn the sleep chart, now Sleepy Star, into a mobile application and learned software development to build it. It is Nicole's goal to help families be the best that they can be every day. It starts with a good nights sleep!
