About Me

I am Nicole DAgnenica, a graphic artist & web developer based in Queens NY. My work experience includes over eight years in product development, calendar managment, branding, design and color for a major retailer in New York City. 



In 2018 I completed a digital art & design online course at S.U.N.Y. Empire State College and an Intensive Web development certificate course at Codecademy which gave me the tools to create projects such as those shown in the portfolio section on this website, in addition to my own portfolio website.

 

I am currently enrolled in the Flatiron's school Software Engineering program learning front and back end web application development. I have a passion for technology and design and continue to enjoy developing my skills in this exciting environment.
