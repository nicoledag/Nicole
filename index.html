I attempted to respectfully hijack a Jackson Pollock canvas, titled One: Number 31 (1950), by utilizing the artist’s ground-breaking style and technique to show potential impacts from oil spills.

About Me

I am Nicole DAgnenica, a freelance graphic artist & web developer based in Queens NY, with a passion for technology and design. My work experience includes over eight years in product development, calendar managment, branding, design and color for a major retailer in New York City. 



In 2018 I completed a digital art & design online course at S.U.N.Y. Empire State College and an Intensive Web development certificate course at Codecademy which gave me the tools to create projects such as those shown in the portfolio section on this website, in addition to my own portfolio website.

 

I continue to enjoy developing my skills in this exciting environment of digital art & design and web development.
