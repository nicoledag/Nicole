About Me

I am Nicole D’Agnenica, a freelance graphic artist & designer based in Queens NY, with appreciation for the arts and diversity of the city and its people and cultures.  



In 2018 I completed a digital art & design online course at S.U.N.Y. Empire State College which gave me the tools to create digital designs in Photoshop for projects such as those shown in the portfolio of designs on this website.

 

I continue to enjoy developing my skills in this exciting environment of digital arts & design.
